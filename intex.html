chicken fried rice recipe

15 min restaurant style recipe


INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 table spoon water
  • 1 table spoon butter
  • 1 table spoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice,cold
  • 1cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 table spoon soy sauce
  • 1 table spoon ground black pepper

Directions

Step 1

gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Beat egg and water together in small bowl. butter in a large skillet or work over medium-low heat;add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes. remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds;set aside

step 3

heat oil in the same skillet. add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce,and pepper. cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg. serve hot.
